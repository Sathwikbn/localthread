# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction
A warm minimalist, multi-vendor marketplace aesthetic for LocalThread featuring cream backgrounds (#FAF9F6), slate black typography (#0F172A), and organic copper/amber highlights (#C2410C). The experience prioritizes shop discovery and local boutique storytelling, incorporating Apple-style clean lines, smooth card physics, and refined typography.

## Reference Points
- Apple (ultra-clean, interactive cards, smooth micro-interactions)
- Aesop/Zara Home (warm minimalist tone, editorial storytelling)
